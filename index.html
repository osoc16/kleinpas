What is 'Kleinpas'?

Kleinpas is a service that helps you remember to book a babysitter a week before your cultural event!
This way, you will never again forget get a babysitter!
The Kleinpas project is a cooperation between Cultuurconnect and the students of oSoc.

What is 'oSoc'?

Open Summer of code is a 4 week programme in different Belgian cities during the summer of 2016 and is part of Open Knowledge Belgium.
The goal: provide Belgian based students the training, network and support necessary to transform open innovation projects into powerful real-world services..

What is 'Cultuurconnect'?

Cultuurconnect is a labo where libraries, cultural and community centres try solutions for digital challenges in the local cultural strategy.
